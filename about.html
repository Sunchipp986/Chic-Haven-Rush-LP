Our Story

At Chic-Haven Rush, we’re more than a garage—we’re a community fueled by speed, precision, and passion. Founded by Shicane Wheeler and co-founded by Ben “Sunchipp” Morris, our mission is to create a haven for automotive enthusiasts who crave adrenaline and innovation.

Our headquarters features a dirt test track, perfect for tuning, experimenting, and pushing vehicles to their limits. Our secondary garage boasts a race track, designed for high-speed laps and skill refinement. Most recently, we expanded to a third location, featuring a drag strip for those who thrive on straight-line speed and head-to-head competition.

At Chic-Haven Rush, every vehicle, every lap, and every thrill is a testament to our commitment to performance, craftsmanship, and the pure joy of racing. Whether you’re testing, racing, or just exploring, we’re here to fuel your automotive dreams.
